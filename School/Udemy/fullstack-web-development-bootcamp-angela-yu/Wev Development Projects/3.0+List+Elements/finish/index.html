My Recipe for the Best Cinamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • ¾ cup of warm water
  • 2¼ teaspoons yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling

  • ½ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ¼ tablespoons ground cinamon
  • ½ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into the dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into the large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for two hours
  6. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinamon
  8. Spread the filling onto the dough and roll the dough into a swiss roll
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a baking tray
  10. Pre-heat the oven to 350F or 180C, then bake the rolls for 20-25min until lightly brown
